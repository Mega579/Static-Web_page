SRI HARMANDIR SAHIB  

 Sri Harmandir Sahib, also known as the Golden Temple, is the holiest Sikh gurdwara (house of worship) in the world. It is located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India. The temple is famous for its stunning golden dome and its beautiful white marble architecture. The Golden Temple was built in the 16th century by Guru Arjan Dev, the fifth Sikh Guru. The temple is located in the middle of a large pool of water, known as the Amrit Sarovar (Pool of Nectar). Sikhs believe that the water in the Amrit Sarovar has healing powers. Every day, thousands of people from all over the world visit the Golden Temple to pray and to experience its peaceful atmosphere.


   BIBI KA MAQBARA   

 Bibi Ka Maqbara, also known as the Mini Taj Mahal, is a beautiful white marble mausoleum located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, India. It was built in the 17th century by Mughal prince Azam Shah in memory of his beloved mother, Dilras Banu Begum. The mausoleum is a stunning example of Mughal architecture, with its intricate carvings, ornate domes, and lush gardens. The main dome is surrounded by four smaller domes, and the entire structure is set on a raised platform. The interior of the mausoleum is just as impressive, with its finely decorated walls and ceilings.

  CHARMINAR  

 Charminar, also known as the "Four Minarets", is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah to commemorate the end of a plague that had struck the city. Charminar is a stunning example of Indo-Islamic architecture, with its intricate carvings, ornate domes, and four towering minarets. The monument is also a popular tourist destination, offering panoramic views of the city from its top floor.


  CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI  

 Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST), formerly known as Victoria Terminus (VT), is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Mumbai, India. It is one of the busiest railway stations in India and is known for its stunning Victorian Gothic Revival architecture. The terminus was built in the 19th century and is a popular tourist destination. CST is a major hub for both long-distance and suburban trains and serves millions of passengers each year. The terminus is also a major landmark in Mumbai and is a symbol of the city's rich history and culture.


  KHAJURAHO  

 Khajuraho is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Madhya Pradesh, India, known for its stunning Hindu and Jain temples. Built in the 10th and 11th centuries, the temples are adorned with intricate carvings, including depictions of erotic art. Khajuraho is a popular tourist destination and is a must-see for anyone interested in Indian history and culture. The temples are a testament to the skill and artistry of the Indian people and are a truly unique and awe-inspiring sight.
